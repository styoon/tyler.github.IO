Breakdown

Even without knowing the exact format of the Spanish Final, you do know that it's going to be comprised of mostly Vocab and Grammar. In the past semester, there have been 5 Spanish Chapters. The Spanish Vocab should be split evenly among the 5 chapters. Assuming that there are 100 words.

Vocab

The joy of having a test on vocab is that you only have to memorize the words. That may sound like a daunting task at first, but it only seems difficult.
